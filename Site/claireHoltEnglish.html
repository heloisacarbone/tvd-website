Claire Holt

Go to the character Rebekah

  • Claire Holt was born in Brisbane, Australia, in June 11, 1988.

  • She graduated from Stuartholme School in Toowong at the end of 2005. She is involved in several sports: swimming, volleyball, water polo player, and Tae-Kwon-Do, in which she has a black belt. When she was younger, she was in the school choir.

  • She is best known for her role as Emma Gilbert on the television show H2O: Just Add

  • She did some films as Mean Girls 2 and Messengers 2: The Scarecrow.

  • Nowadays, she's been acting as Rebekah in The Vampire Diaries, and as Samara in The Pretty Little Liars.
