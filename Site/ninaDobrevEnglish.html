  • Nina Constantinova Dobrev, was born in Sofia, Bulgaria in january 9, 1989.
  • She is an actress, singer, player and model that lives in Canada, nowadays.
  • Act in the "The Vampire Diaries" serie as Elena Gilbert and Katherine Petrova.
  • Nina moved to Canada with two years old and lives in Toronto, Ontario, since them.
  • Started working as a model in public advertising, and next started doing auditions to movies.
  • Some time later, she got roles in some movies, as "Fugitive Pieces", "Away from Her", "Never Cry Werewolf", and on the tv serie "Degrassi: The Next Generation". Also having the main character in "The vampire Diaries".
