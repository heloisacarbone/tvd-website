  • Joseph Morgan, borned in 16 May 1981, is an English actor.

  • He lived in Morriston, Swansea for 11 years[2] and he was a pupil at Morriston Comprehensive School before moving back to London to study at the Central School of Speech and Drama in his late teens.

  • He starred in the first series of the Sky One television series Hex, as Troy and has appeared in supporting roles in some films as "Alexander" and "Master and Commander: The Far Side of the World".

  • Nowadays he has been cast as the original vampire Klaus in The CW's hit show The Vampire Diaries.
