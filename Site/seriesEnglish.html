The Vampire Diaries is an american tv series of horror, 
  fantasy and romance developed by Kevin Williamson and based 
  on the book collection wrote by L. J. Smith. 
  The series tell the events that happens in the Mystic Falls city, Virginia.
  A fictional small town haunted by supernatural beings. 
  The climax  of the series is the lovely triangle between the
   main characters, Elena Gilbert(Nina Dobrev) and the Salvatore's 
   brothers (Paul Wesley and Ian Somerhalder).
