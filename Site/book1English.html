The Awekening - 1991

In "The Awekening", first book from the collection. The loveing 
triangle more astonishing for ever. Two vampire brothers, a good and
 a malign, are in love for the same human. Stefan, the good one, knew
Elena and fell in love for her momentarily, but swore never be closed 
to her, due to his gloomy secret. However, Elena swore attract Stefan 
– or die.


“The Awekening” is the first book of the collection, originally
 published in 1991.


Best seller of the series Vampire Diaries, came to be more than 
15 weeks on the best seller list on the New York Times.
