First Season

In Mystic Falls, Virginia, Elena Gilbert (Nina Dobrev)
a teenager of 17 years, that has lost her parents in a car accident. 
She and her brother, Jeremy Gilbert (Steven R. McQueen) will live with 
their aunt, Jenna Sommers (Sara Canning), at the old house of 
their parents. When they return to school, Elena falls in love with a
 vampire called Stefan Salvatore (Paul Wesley). 
Neverthless their life turned more and more complicated, 
because Stefan's brother, Damon Salvatore (Ian Somerhalder) is back
 to the city and want revenge. The brothers fall in love with Elena, 
 mainly because of her resemblance with their old love, Katherine Pierce 
 (Nina Dobrev). Bonnie Bennett (Katerina Graham) a witch, who is the
  best friend of Elena, try to be over of everything that involves vampires, 
  but in the end, she always have to help them.
